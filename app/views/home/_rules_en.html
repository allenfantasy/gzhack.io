1. Fill in the application form to sign up and submit personal information. The competition committee will then select 200 final participants in consideration of different background, region and specialty of the applicants.
  2. Each participant will have the opportunity to present his/her idea to all participants in the opening day, after which will be the free teaming up time.
  3. Participants are given 48 hours to develop their product prototypes.
  4. A 3-minute demonstration of the product prototypes given to the judges and audiences at the final stage can be done through a real production operation, a PPT, a video, or a speech. The judges will make the final decision on the winner for each award.
